Falling and Rising Wedges

Rising Wedge

The resistance line formed by higher highs

The support line is formed by lower lows

The support and resistance lines converge towards each other

Consistent bullish pattern





Falling Wedge

The resistance line is formed by lower highs

The support line is formed by lower lows

The support and resistance lines converge towards each other

Consistent bearish pattern, nearly identical to a double top
