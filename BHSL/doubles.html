Double Bottoms and Tops

Double Bottoms

The neckline aka resistance line is formed by the single high from a bounce from the first low

The support line is formed by two equivalent lows

Extremely bullish pattern





Double Tops

The resistance line is formed by two equivalent highs

The neckline aka support line is formed by the single low from a rejection off the first high

Extremely bearish pattern
